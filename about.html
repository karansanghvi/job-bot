About JobMatchBot

JobMatchBot is an AI-powered resume-job matching tool that uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques like:

  • TF-IDF for keyword-based similarity
  • BERT embeddings for contextual understanding
  • Feedback generation to show matched and missing skills

It's built using Python, Flask, Bootstrap, and open-source NLP libraries. The tool helps job seekers optimize their resumes by comparing them directly to job descriptions and receiving detailed keyword insights.
